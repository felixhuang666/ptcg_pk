Objective:當使用者放大或是縮小, 要重新計算地圖的view, 要以當前的位置作為中心, 來調整

**Original Branch Commit ID:**
N/A

**Root Cause:**
The original zoom logic in `RpgMode.tsx` simply called `this.cameras.main.setZoom(newZoom)` on wheel or pinch events. In Phaser, this scales the camera around its default focal point (usually the center of the viewport), which causes the map to appear to drift away from the user's cursor or touch point when zooming.

**Solution:**
Implemented a `performZoom` function that calculates the world point of the pointer (or midpoint of pinch) before and after applying the new zoom. It then adjusts the camera's `scrollX` and `scrollY` by the difference, effectively anchoring the zoom to that specific point in the world. Additionally, to prevent the camera from immediately snapping back to the player while zooming, `this.cameras.main.stopFollow()` is called during the zoom action, and `this.cameras.main.startFollow()` is resumed as soon as the player moves again.
